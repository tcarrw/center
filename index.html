Scottie Pippen’s Underwater Pets

In the deep blue gym, a seahorse dribbles bubbles.
A manta glides the length of memory — assist after assist.
Jellyfish lanterns float through old Chicago light.

Scottie feeds them silence and leftover highlight reels.
They circle like teammates who never retired.
Even here, gravity listens to defense.

The coral keep score in whispers.
The scoreboard blinks “breathe.”
Every rebound is a wave returning home.
