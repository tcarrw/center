Bridge of Joy 路 Dagny

Before the lesson, she laughs.

before the bell, she dances.

joy is not taught here. it arrives,
buzzing through open windows,
pink light in her wake.

when you hear her song, remember:
the heart learns first.

hum once. enter when it blooms.
